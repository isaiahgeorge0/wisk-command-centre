# WISK — Product Vision

## What is WISK?

WISK is a business command centre for ambitious 
people.

It is built for the entrepreneur managing five 
client projects and a content strategy. The 
creator with a hundred ideas and no system to 
capture them. The small business owner juggling 
leads, tasks, goals, and team members across 
tools that do not talk to each other. The agency 
founder who needs visibility across everything 
without opening twelve tabs.

WISK is the single place where a user can plan, 
manage, execute, understand, and control their 
business — and eventually aspects of their 
personal life.

The goal is not to be another productivity app. 
The goal is to become the operating system for 
how ambitious people run their lives and 
businesses. The type of product where users 
eventually wonder how they ever operated without 
it.

## Who is WISK for?

WISK is built for people who have more going on 
than most tools are designed to handle.

The primary user is someone operating at the 
intersection of creativity and business. They 
have projects, clients, ideas, content, goals, 
and a team — all moving simultaneously. They are 
not looking for a simple to-do list. They need 
a command centre.

Think of someone like Kanye West — an individual 
with so many creative ideas, business ventures, 
collaborators, and moving parts that without a 
true source of truth, things fall through the 
cracks. WISK is that source of truth.

**Primary users today:**
- Freelancers and independent developers
- Content creators and social media managers
- Small business owners
- Creative agency founders
- Entrepreneurs managing multiple ventures

**Users as WISK grows:**
- Small landlords managing properties and tenants
- Marketing agencies managing client work and teams
- Any ambitious individual who needs one place 
  for everything

## The core problem WISK solves

Most people run their business across five to 
ten different tools. Tasks in one place. Leads 
in another. Content calendar somewhere else. 
Goals written in a notebook. Ideas in voice 
memos. Revenue tracked in a spreadsheet.

The cost is not just switching between tools. 
The cost is the connections that never get made. 
The lead that never became a project. The idea 
that never became content. The goal that was 
set in January and forgotten by March.

WISK solves this by making everything visible 
in one place and making the connections between 
things explicit. A lead becomes a project. A 
project generates tasks. Tasks connect to goals. 
Goals drive content. Content generates new leads.

## What makes WISK different

Most productivity tools are built for everyone, 
which means they are built for no one in 
particular.

WISK is different in three ways:

**1. Modular subscriptions that unlock vertical 
power**

The core WISK product is genuinely useful for 
any business owner or creator. But the packages 
are what give WISK its long-term potential.

A small landlord pays for the Property package 
and gets tenant management, maintenance 
workflows, and contractor assignment — all 
connected to their existing projects and tasks.

A content creator pays for the Social package 
and gets platform analytics, AI-powered 
performance insights, competitor tracking, and 
content scheduling — all connected to their 
existing content calendar and goals.

Each package solves a specific high-value problem 
for a specific type of user. The core platform 
is the foundation. The packages are the power.

**2. Intelligence that gets smarter over time**

WISK is not just a place to store information. 
It is a place that understands your information 
and helps you act on it.

As users add more data — more projects, tasks, 
leads, content, goals — the AI layer becomes 
more useful. It surfaces patterns. It flags 
risks before they become problems. It suggests 
what to do next based on what is actually 
happening in the business.

**3. Built for ambition, not just organisation**

Most productivity tools help you manage what 
you have. WISK is designed to help you build 
what you want. Goals with progress tracking. 
Leads that become revenue. Content that builds 
an audience. Ideas that become products.

## The long-term vision

In three years WISK is a worldwide productivity 
platform used by individuals and small teams 
across multiple sectors.

The core product serves any ambitious business 
owner or creator. The packages serve specific 
verticals — property, social media, agencies, 
and more. The AI layer ties everything together, 
providing intelligence and automation that no 
single-purpose tool can match.

WISK is built by Isaiah George Creative under 
the WISK umbrella — a family of products that 
share a common platform, common design language, 
and common belief that ambitious people deserve 
tools built for their level of ambition.

## What WISK is not

WISK is not a project management tool for 
enterprise. It is not trying to compete with 
Jira or Asana for large organisations.

WISK is not a simple to-do list. It is not 
Todoist or Tick Tick.

WISK is not a note-taking app. It is not Notion 
trying to be everything to everyone.

WISK is a command centre for ambitious people 
who are building something. That focus is what 
makes it powerful.
